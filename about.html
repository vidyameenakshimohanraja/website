Vidya Meenakshi

I'm Vidya. I grew up in India and at 17 moved to Birmingham to study English Literature and Creative Writing. From there I relocated to Singapore for a Masters in Strategic and Data Communications at NUS. Moving between countries from such a young age became normal pretty quickly and I have learned to adapt, figure things out fast and get comfortable being the new person in the room. That shapes how I work every day.

And when I am not working, I am making something. I love being creative and messy, thinking outside the box, finding solutions, automating things and thinking freely. I write poetry and I read a lot — mainly gothic fiction, slice of life and historical. In my free time I draw on my iPad, make digital art on Canva and animate them, and sometimes I build tiny 3D creatures on Blender. But at the end of the day there is nothing better than rewatching my favourite film at night with a warm cookie and milk.
